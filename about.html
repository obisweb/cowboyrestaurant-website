About Us

CowBoy Restaurant Steak House Restaurant

Unlike the steak house in shopping mall or high end restaurant; Old Western Cowboy Steak House; 西域牛仔扒座 is a local style or mamak style steak house serving cheap and affordable western food. However, it still attracts a number of customers visiting for simple yet delicious dinner, drink and hangout. Black Pepper Lamb Chop, Marmalade Chicken Chop and Sizzling Fish and Chips gain good feedback from most of the customer. Surprisingly, the steakhouse serves Escargot as well. Desa Petaling "Old Western Cowboy Steak House" is a Chinese and Western restaurant with a great history. Throughout the years of existence since 2007, it has been loved and praised by countless customers. In the Restaurant and Food Industry: We are one of the recommended restaurants by ASTRO AEC in May 2020. By offering both Western and Chinese cuisine has definitely satisfied many customers taste buds. For Western food it has a signature marinated steak with exclusive sauce and gravy alongside with exclusive Chinese food series.

For the convenience of everyone, Our Restaurant specially prepares vacuum-frozen Chinese and Western food "Cowboy Cold Storage" for everyone~. Our mission is to provide the best food and service to everyone.
